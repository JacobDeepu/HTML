Govt. Polytechnic College Cherthala is one of the most prestigious and premier Polytechnic colleges in Kerala. It is an institute that offers a variety of professional courses which are technical in nature. The courses offered at GPTC help an individual to kick-start his career. They allow him to start his own business or get a decent job at a reputed place. The college has state-of-the-art laboratories, workshops and hi-tech library having large variety of books, periodicals, national and international journals to meet the demands of today as well as future.
